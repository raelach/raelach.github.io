Who is Spongebob Squarepants?
Spongebob Squarepants is a yellow sponge from Bikini Bottom who has generated many dank memes and GIFS. He lives in a pineapple under the sea with his snail Gary and works as a fry cook at the Krusty Krab. Other main characters include his bestfriend Patrick the starfish and Squidward Tentacles, Spongebob's neighbour.
